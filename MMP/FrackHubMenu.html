What Is FrackHub?


FrackHub is an environment designed to rent item's to and from people in your local community.

Do you have a lawnmower that you never use?
why not rent it out to someone in your community in return for credits which you can use to rent items yourself.



How Do You Use FrackHub?


Upload Items

Any item you would like to rent out, first has to be uploaded to FrackHub, this involves filling in a short form about your item.

My Items

Once You have uploaded an item, it can be viewed in the My Items section, this area shows all your items that you have uploaded. If the item is being rented out it will show you details on who has possession of your item. If your item is waiting to be rented out it will show all the offers that have been made for the item.

Search Items

This area, allows you to explore other users items, and make them offers to rent their items

My Items Rented

If another user accepts one of your offers you have made to them, you will now be renting their item, any item that you have rented from other users will show up in this section.
